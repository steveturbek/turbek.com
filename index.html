"Happy Feet" interactive scultupure

(Shown at Art Bots 2003) Happy Feet is an installation of 5 pairs of elegant footwear. Each shoe is mechanically articulated, enabling it to tap. The shoes are then free to dance, to create chorus line patterns, to interact with the audience. When unattended, the microcontroller (hidden inside shoeboxes) caused the articulated shoes to tap rhythms. When a person steps on the foot pads, the shoes copy their steps.
