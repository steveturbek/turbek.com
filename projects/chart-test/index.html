Charts: The game

How good are you at reading charts? Which chart is the best?

Guess the amount of each of the charts

All answers are 2 digit numbers

  • For Line, Column, Point, and Bar charts, guess the amount
  • For Pie Charts, guess the Percentage (of 100%) of the selected slice
  • To guess faster, you can hit enter / return instead of clicking the button

Your accuracy will be averaged, and compared with everyone playing the game. I'll publish the effectiveness of each chart on stephen.turbek.com or follow @stephenturbek on twitter.
