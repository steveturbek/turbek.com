    Help making a Survee survey

    1. Write your questions
      • Questions are shown one at a time, starting with a Welcome slide and ending with a Thank You slide
      • Edit any text by clicking on it and typing
      • Change a Question's Type with the Question Type menu
      • Delete Answers by clicking
      • Move around the survey with the links on the left hand side (or use the Next and Back buttons)
      • Click 'Style this Survey' to change the colors of the survey
      • Done? Send out the survey (Survey invitations from this site are coming soon)
    2. Read the results
      • The survey results will be visible here after the first survey response is recorded.
